A Life Remembered

His story, in our hearts

Timothy A. Thomas, a devoted husband, father, grandfather, brother, and accomplished attorney and business professional, entered eternal rest on Monday, June 15, 2026 with his beloved wife Beryl, by his side and surrounded by the love of family and friends.

His life was marked by academic excellence, professional achievement, deep personal relationships, and a commitment to service and leadership.
